# Executive Retail Performance Dashboard | Power BI

## Business Context

A growing retail business often generates large volumes of sales data but struggles to answer critical business questions:

- Which products generate the highest profit?
- Which products consistently lose money?
- Are discounts improving sales or reducing profitability?
- Which regions deserve more investment?
- Where is profit leaking?

Without a centralized dashboard, management spends valuable time creating reports instead of making decisions.

---

## Objective

Build an interactive executive dashboard that enables business leaders to monitor sales performance, identify profit leakage, and make faster data-driven decisions.

---

## Business Questions Solved

This dashboard answers the questions executives ask every day:

- How much revenue and profit have we generated?
- Which categories contribute the highest profit?
- Which products are causing the biggest losses?
- Which region generates the highest revenue?
- How are discounts affecting profitability?
- What actions should management take to improve performance?

---

## Executive KPIs

- Revenue
- Profit
- Total Orders
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Sales Growth %
- Profit Margin %

---

## Dashboard Features

### Executive Overview
Provides a high-level summary of overall business performance using key KPIs and executive insights.

### Product Performance Analysis
Highlights:

- Top-performing products
- Loss-making products
- Category-wise revenue
- Category-wise profit

### Regional Performance
Analyze revenue across regions to identify top-performing markets.

### Discount Analysis
Understand how discounting impacts profitability and identify opportunities to improve pricing strategy.

### Monthly Performance Trends
Track sales and profit trends over multiple years to understand seasonality and business growth.

### Business Recommendations
The dashboard concludes with actionable recommendations that help management prioritize decisions instead of simply viewing data.

---

# Key Insights

Based on the Sample Superstore dataset:

- Technology generates the highest overall profit.
- Furniture produces strong revenue but significantly lower margins.
- Several products consistently generate negative profit.
- Higher discount levels are frequently associated with lower profitability.
- The West region contributes the highest share of revenue.

---

# Business Recommendations

- Review products with discounts above 30%.
- Investigate pricing strategy for loss-making products.
- Increase focus on high-margin categories such as Technology.
- Reassess low-margin Furniture products.
- Continuously monitor negative-margin SKUs.
